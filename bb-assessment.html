How it works

Manuscript Assessments

This type of assessment is best suited to more experienced authors whose work we have previously assessed or who have other publications.

Writers who request Manuscript assessments usually have a completed second or third draft of a manuscript or book proposal and are not sure what to do next.

In a Manuscript assessment we evaluate your manuscript to identify its strengths and weaknesses from a publishing point of view, considering marketability, readability, potential readership, and structural strengths and weaknesses.

A Manuscript assessment will provide advice about how you can best go about developing the manuscript to publishable standard, and what types of publication it might be appropriate to aim for.

Our Manuscript assessments also detail specific areas where reviewing, reworking and rewriting may be required to bring a work to publishable standard. Where appropriate, we will also provide advice on possible marketing strategies and information on how to approach publishers and agents.

A Manuscript assessment will include copy editing of the first chapter. It may tell you that your work needs further editing or proofreading, but it won’t provide this service for you.

What do I get for my money?

Our assessment reports are detailed, extensive and thorough. They vary in length from around 2500 words to above 5000, depending on how detailed our comments need to be for individual manuscripts.

All our reports address the specific concerns of the author and include an analysis of the areas that we feel have the greatest need for improvement. We cite specific examples and offer possible alternatives on how to approach making these changes.

That said, we will not teach you how to rewrite your manuscript, and unless you have requested a copy edit, we will only copy edit the first chapter.

How long will it take for you to asses my manuscript?

Manuscripts generally take between four and six weeks for assessment, depending on our workload. We will advise you of the approximate turn-around time for your work on submission.
